Red Lentil Pasta
Red lentil pasta, made from red lentil flour, is a nutritious, gluten-free alternative to traditional pasta. Packed with protein, fiber, and essential nutrients, its vibrant color and earthy flavor pair well with sauces like marinara and pesto.
